About Us

IMPS(Institute of Management & Placement Studies)College of Engineering & Technology popularly known as IMPSCET,situated at Malda,established in 2002,is one of the top ranked engineering college in North Bengal.It is committed to deliver high quality technical knowledge.It is affiliated to MAKAUT (formerly known as WBUT) and AICTE

It is a part of group named 'IMPS Educational Trust' which includes:

  • IMPS College of Engineering & Technology
  • Imps Polytechnic college
  • DOON Heritage School
  • A hotel
  • It & Management institute

 

Program Offered :

B.Tech-Computer Science and Engineering - 4Years.
